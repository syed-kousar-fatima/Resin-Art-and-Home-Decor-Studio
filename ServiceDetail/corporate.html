Our Process

From Brief to Delivery —
A Seamless Journey

We make the corporate gifting process effortless, from first enquiry to branded boxes arriving at your door or your recipient’s desk.

01

Discovery Call & Brief

We begin with a 20-minute discovery call or email exchange to understand your brand, occasion, budget, recipient profile, and key messaging. We ask the right questions so nothing is left to chance. No obligation — the call is always free.

Day 1
02

Design Proposal & Sampling

Within 48 hours we send a visual design proposal showing your brand colours in resin, logo placement options, and packaging mockups. For orders over 50 units, a physical sample is produced and couriered to you for approval at no extra charge.

Day 2–3
03

Quote, Approval & Deposit

We issue a full itemised quote including unit cost, branding, packaging, and delivery. Once you approve, a 50% deposit secures your production slot. We assign a dedicated account manager to your order from this point forward.

Day 3–5
04

Artisan Production

Your order enters our production queue. Resin is mixed, poured, layered, and cured over multiple stages. Logo or text embedding is done during the final pour layer for flush, professional results. Each unit is individually inspected before packaging.

Day 5–14
05

Branded Packaging

Completed pieces are individually wrapped in tissue paper, placed in your branded gift box (custom printed or generic premium), and sealed with a branded sticker or ribbon. Multi-item hampers are assembled, photographed, and checked before dispatch.

Day 14–16
06

Delivery & After-Sales

Orders are dispatched via tracked courier — single address or direct-to-recipient drop-ship across the UK. We email you a tracking dashboard. Balance payment is due on dispatch. All corporate orders include a 6-month quality guarantee.

Day 16–20
Quality & Presentation

Premium Products & Branded Packaging

Every element of your corporate gift order is considered — from the resin formula to the final ribbon tied on the box.

Crystal-Clear Epoxy

UV-stable, non-yellowing epoxy resin with a glass-smooth surface. Rated for 50+ years without discolouration — your brand stays vibrant and impactful for years to come.

Custom Branded Boxes

Choose from magnetic-close rigid boxes, kraft paper folding boxes, or premium drawer boxes — all printable with your logo, brand colours, and custom messaging inside the lid.

Gold & Silver Leaf

24-carat gold leaf and 99.9% silver leaf embedded within the resin surface. Adds luxurious texture and glimmer that signals premium quality with every glance.

Logo Embedding

Your vector logo is embedded directly into the resin pour — flush, durable, and impossible to peel or scratch. Available in full colour, metallic gold, or white silhouette.

Direct-to-Recipient Delivery

We can ship individual branded gifts directly to each of your recipients across the UK — ideal for remote teams, client appreciation campaigns, and distributed event gifts.

Quality Guarantee

Every unit is inspected individually before dispatch. Our 6-month corporate quality guarantee covers any defects — we replace units at no cost, no questions asked.
