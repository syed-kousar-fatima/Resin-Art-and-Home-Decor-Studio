Our Craft

How Every Piece
Is Made By Hand

Jewellery making at ResinLux is a slow, intentional process. No shortcuts — every step matters to the final result.

01

Design Consultation

We start with a personal conversation about your vision — occasion, colour palette, inclusions (botanicals, crystals, ashes, gold leaf), preferred metal tone, and whether the piece is for you or a gift. For bridal and memorial commissions, we have a dedicated private consultation process.

Day 1
02

Inclusion Sourcing & Preparation

Botanical inclusions are pressed and dried for a minimum of 2 weeks before use to preserve natural colour and shape. For customer-provided flowers or botanicals (e.g. wedding bouquets), we guide you on how to prepare and send them. Gemstones, gold leaf, and crystal shards are sourced from verified ethical suppliers.

Day 2–3
03

Resin Mixing & Pigment Blending

High-quality, jewellery-grade epoxy resin is measured to exacting ratios and blended with your chosen pigments. Jewellery resin is a specific formulation — harder, clearer, and more scratch-resistant than art resin. Colour depth is built layer by layer for maximum vibrancy and translucency.

Day 3–4
04

Moulding, Pouring & Setting Inclusions

Resin is carefully poured into precision silicone moulds or directly into metal bezels. Inclusions are placed by hand with jeweller’s tweezers at precise depths to ensure they float beautifully at the right position. Multiple thin pours are used to build depth without trapping air bubbles.

Day 4–6
05

Sanding, Polishing & Finishing

After full cure, each piece is wet-sanded through 6 grit stages (220 to 5000) until optically clear. A UV-resistant resin topcoat or jewellery polish is applied for glass-like clarity and scratch resistance. Metal components are cleaned, polished, and assembled by hand in our jewellery studio.

Day 6–8
06

Gifting, Packaging & Delivery

Your jewellery is nestled on a velvet cushion inside a magnetic-close luxury gift box, with a care card, a certificate of authenticity, and a handwritten note if requested. Sent via tracked delivery with full insurance. Estimated 8–12 working days from order to doorstep.

Day 8–12
Premium Materials

What Goes Into Every Piece

Only jewellery-grade, hypoallergenic materials are used — chosen for clarity, durability, and lasting beauty.

Jewellery-Grade Resin

Crystal-clear, UV-stable, ultra-hard epoxy resin formulated specifically for jewellery. Scratch-resistant, waterproof, and rated for daily wear. BPA-free and hypoallergenic.

24-Carat Gold Leaf

Genuine 24-carat gold and 99.9% silver leaf applied within the resin layers. Adds luxurious warmth, sparkle, and depth that catches light beautifully at every angle.

Real Botanicals & Florals

Pressed and dried florals, leaves, ferns, and petals from your own bouquet or our curated studio collection. Preserved permanently inside the resin with full colour retention.

Crystal & Gemstone Inclusions

Crushed amethyst, rose quartz, turquoise powder, and other semi-precious stone fragments available as inclusions — adding natural colour, sparkle, and meaning to each piece.

Sterling Silver & Gold-Fill

All metal findings, bezels, hooks, and chains are sterling silver (925), 14k gold-fill, or rose gold-fill. Nickel-free and hypoallergenic — safe for sensitive skin and daily wear.

Luxury Gift Packaging

Every piece is presented on a velvet cushion in a magnetic-close gift box with tissue paper, a ribbon, a handwritten note option, and a care & storage guide card included as standard.
