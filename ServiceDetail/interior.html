Project Journey

From Site Visit to
Stunning Installation

A rigorous, transparent process ensures every installation is flawless — from the first measurement to the final polish.

01

Free Site Visit & Measurement

One of our project leads visits your space to take precise measurements, assess substrate conditions, lighting, temperature, and humidity. We photograph the space and discuss your vision in depth. No charge, no obligation. We cover residential and commercial sites across the UK.

Week 1
02

Design Concept & 3D Visualisation

Our design team produces a full colour concept with detailed measurements, material specifications, finish options, and a 3D render of how the installation will look in your specific space. Multiple revisions are included. You approve before any work begins.

Week 1–2
03

Quote, Approval & Scheduling

A full itemised quote is issued — covering materials, labour, delivery, and installation. A 40% deposit secures your production and installation slot. Typical lead time is 3–6 weeks depending on project scale. A dedicated project manager oversees your job from this point.

Week 2
04

Studio Fabrication

For tables, panels, and prefabricated elements, production begins in our workshop. Resin is mixed, layered, poured over multiple sessions, and cured under controlled temperature. Wood slabs are kiln-dried, machined, and fitted with structural supports before resin application. Each stage is photographed and shared with you.

Weeks 3–5
05

On-Site Installation

Our specialist installation crew arrives at your property. For in-situ pours (bar tops, floors, walls), the surface is prepared, primed, and sealed before resin is applied. For prefabricated elements (tables, panels), our team handles white-glove delivery and professional fixing. Work areas are fully protected throughout.

Weeks 5–6
06

Finishing, Handover & Aftercare

Final sanding, polishing, and UV topcoat application ensures a mirror-perfect surface. We complete a full quality inspection with you present, hand over your care guide and maintenance kit, and register your 10-year structural guarantee. Follow-up service visits are included in the first year.

Technical Excellence

Materials Built to Last Decades

We specify only structural-grade, professionally-sourced materials for every installation — chosen for performance, beauty, and longevity.

Structural Epoxy Resin

Industrial-grade, 100% solids epoxy with Shore D hardness of 80+. UV-stable, non-yellowing, rated to 200°C. Certified for structural and food-contact use in commercial environments.

Kiln-Dried Solid Wood Slabs

Sustainably sourced English walnut, American black walnut, white oak, and elm slabs dried to below 8% moisture for dimensional stability. Every slab is hand-selected for grain character.

24-Carat Gold & Metal Inclusions

Gold leaf, copper powder, mica metallic pigments, and crushed quartz embedded within resin layers for texture, depth, and the distinctive ResinLux aesthetic of luxury and artistry.

UV-Resistant Topcoats

Professional-grade UV-resistant varnishes applied in multiple coats after full cure. Prevents yellowing from sunlight exposure for a minimum of 15 years, maintaining colour vibrancy throughout.

Steel & Structural Hardware

All tables include powder-coated steel or solid brass legs engineered for the specific weight of the slab. Wall fixings use hidden French cleats rated to 10× the load of the installation.

Anti-Slip & Specialist Coatings

Floors and staircase treads receive anti-slip aggregate finishes rated R11–R13. Bathroom and spa surfaces are treated with anti-microbial coatings. All finishes are independently tested to British Standards.
