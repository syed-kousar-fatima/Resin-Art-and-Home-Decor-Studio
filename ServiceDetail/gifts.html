The Process

From Your Idea to Their
Perfect Gift

Ordering a personalised ResinLux gift is simple, joyful, and completely guided by our team from start to delivery.

01

Tell Us Your Vision

Complete our online order form with the recipient’s name, the occasion, your preferred colour palette, any inclusions you’d like (botanicals, florals, gold leaf), and your delivery deadline. No idea is too detailed or too open — our gift designers can suggest the perfect piece for any budget.

Day 1
02

Design Confirmation & Quote

Within 24 hours we send a personalised design proposal with colour swatches, product options, and a clear price. For wedding orders with 50+ items, we produce a physical sample at no extra charge so you can approve the exact finish before production begins. Revisions are always free.

Day 1–2
03

Artisan Production

Once you confirm, our artisans begin crafting your gifts by hand. Resin is mixed, pigments are blended to your exact palette, and inclusions are placed with jeweller’s precision. Personalisation (names, dates, messages) is embedded into the final resin layer for a permanent, seamless result.

Days 2–6
04

Quality Check & Finishing

Each piece is individually inspected, wet-sanded to a high-gloss clarity, and finished with a UV-resistant topcoat. Wedding favour sets are batch-checked to ensure perfect colour consistency across every unit. Any piece that doesn’t meet our standard is remade before it reaches you.

Day 6–7
05

Gift Wrapping & Packaging

Every gift is placed in a luxury magnetic-close box, nestled on velvet cushioning with tissue paper and ribbon. Wedding favours are individually wrapped and tied, then packed into presentation trays by table. A handwritten personalised message card is placed inside every single order.

Day 7–8
06

Delivery to Your Door

Dispatched via fully tracked, insured courier. Single gifts arrive in 1–2 working days from dispatch. Wedding favour orders are delivered in custom presentation boxes arranged by table number, ready to place directly at your venue. Next-day delivery available on most single gift orders.

Day 8–10
Every Detail Matters

What’s Included in Every Gift Order

From the first pour to the final ribbon — nothing is left to chance. Every ResinLux gift is complete from the moment it leaves our studio.

Premium Resin & Pigments

UV-stable, non-toxic, food-safe epoxy resin blended with cosmetic-grade mica pigments in over 80 colours. Every gift maintains its vibrancy for decades without yellowing or fading.

Real Botanical Inclusions

Dried flowers, pressed petals, foliage, and botanicals from your own wedding bouquet or our studio collection. Permanently preserved inside the resin, never to fade or wither.

24-Carat Gold Leaf

Genuine 24-carat gold and silver leaf available as a premium inclusion in any piece. Catches the light beautifully and adds a truly luxurious dimension to birthday and wedding gifts alike.

Luxury Gift Box & Ribbon

Every piece is presented in a magnetic-close premium gift box with velvet cushioning, tissue paper, and a satin ribbon. Wedding favours are individually wrapped and arranged. No extra charge for packaging.

Personalised Message Card

Every order includes a handwritten or printed personalised message card from you to your recipient. For wedding favours, individual per-guest name tags are available as part of the order.

Care Guide & Guarantee

All gifts include a printed care and maintenance guide with tips for display, cleaning, and long-term storage. Every piece is covered by a 12-month quality guarantee against defects.
