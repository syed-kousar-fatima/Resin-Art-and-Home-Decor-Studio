What Is Bespoke?

Art Made Entirely
for You

A bespoke commission is the ultimate personalised artwork. Unlike ready-made pieces, every element of a bespoke resin work is designed from scratch to match your space, palette, style, and story.

From a single abstract panel above your sofa to a full dining table set in ocean resin — we collaborate with you at every stage, sharing digital previews and colour samples before a single drop of epoxy is poured.

  • No two pieces ever identical
  • Digital mockup before production begins
  • Any size from A3 to 2m×2m+
  • UV-resistant, heat-stable finish
  • Certificate of authenticity included
  • Nationwide delivery & installation

How It Works

Your Commission Journey,
Step by Step

A collaborative, transparent process from first idea to finished artwork on your wall.

01

Discovery Consultation

We begin with a free 30-minute consultation — in-studio, online, or by phone. We discuss your space, style preferences, colour palette, budget, and timeline.

Day 1
02

Design & Mockup

Our artists create a detailed digital mockup showing colour layout, technique, and scale. You can request revisions freely until you’re 100% happy before we proceed.

Day 2–3
03

Artisan Crafting

Once approved, our artisans begin the pour process. Depending on complexity, this takes 2–7 days — involving multiple layers, curing stages, and precision finishing.

Day 4–10
04

Quality Review & Sanding

Each piece is inspected under studio lighting, hand-sanded through five grits up to 3000, and finished with a UV-resistant gloss topcoat for lasting brilliance.

Day 10–12
05

Delivery & Installation

Your artwork is custom-packaged and dispatched with specialist art couriers. For large pieces, we offer white-glove installation by our team at no extra charge.

Day 13–16
06

Aftercare & Guarantee

Receive your care guide, certificate of authenticity, and 12-month guarantee. We’re always available for advice, touch-ups, or future commissions.

Craft & Quality

Premium Materials & Techniques

Every commission uses gallery-grade materials chosen for longevity, vibrancy, and safety.

Crystal-Clear Epoxy

Food-safe, low-VOC, UV-resistant epoxy resin rated for 50+ years. Self-levels to a glass-smooth finish without bubbles.

Professional Pigments

Cosmetic-grade mica powders, alcohol inks, and custom-blended epoxy pigments in over 200 colours — fade-proof and UV-stable.

Premium Substrates

Marine-grade birch ply, gallery canvas, solid wood slabs, and acrylic boards selected for their adhesion, weight, and longevity.

Advanced Techniques

Ocean pour, dirty pour, Dutch pour, blowtorch cells, lacing, geode inlay, gold leaf application — each chosen to achieve your exact desired look.

Real Inclusions

Crushed quartz crystals, 24-carat gold leaf, real dried botanicals, shells, and glass beads embedded for depth and texture.

Eco-Conscious Process

BPA-free, non-toxic formulations. Sustainable wood sourcing, zero single-use plastic packaging, and recyclable backing boards.
