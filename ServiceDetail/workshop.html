Workshop Curriculum

What You’ll Learn &
Create Step by Step

Every session follows our proven creative curriculum — structured enough to guide, free enough to inspire.

01

Welcome & Introduction (15 min)

Arrive to a beautifully laid studio with your personal workstation fully prepared. Your artist guide introduces the session, explains safety, and shares the day’s creative brief. You choose your personal colour palette from over 80 pigment options and discuss your vision.

Arrival
02

Resin Science & Mixing Ratios (20 min)

Understand the chemistry behind epoxy resin — how to correctly measure and mix your resin and hardener to the right ratio, how to avoid common mistakes like cloudiness, sticky surfaces, and overheating. We demonstrate using temperature, timing, and technique.

Theory
03

Colour Blending & Pigment Play (30 min)

Get hands-on with mixing pigments, mica powders, alcohol inks, and glitter. Learn colour theory as it applies to resin — contrast, harmony, dominance, and flow. Guided exercises help you blend your chosen palette before the main pour begins.

Hands-On
04

The Main Pour & Technique (60–90 min)

The heart of the session. Pour your resin onto your chosen substrate and use your selected technique — ocean pour, dirty pour, Dutch pour, bloom, or geode inlay. Your guide demonstrates each move, then watches and coaches as you create. Blowtorch cells, tilting, and lacing are all covered here.

Main Event
05

Finishing, Curing & Care (20 min)

Learn how to remove bubbles with a blowtorch, cover your piece to cure safely, and apply a UV-resistant topcoat. We explain the full 24–48-hour curing process, how to handle and transport your work, long-term care, and cleaning advice.

Finishing
06

Showcase & Take Home (15 min)

Share and celebrate what you’ve made with the group. Every student receives a care card, a studio keepsake bag, a 10% loyalty discount on future workshops, and — most importantly — their finished artwork to take home proudly.

Wrap Up

You arrive empty-handed and leave with an artwork you’re proud of. Everything in between is on us.

Premium Resin Kit

Crystal-clear, low-VOC epoxy resin and hardener pre-measured to your project. BPA-free, non-toxic, and food-safe formula used by professional resin artists.

80+ Pigment Colours

Cosmetic-grade mica powders, alcohol inks, neon pigments, holographic glitters, and custom metallic blends. Choose freely and experiment to your heart’s content.

Substrate & Canvas

Pre-levelled birch wood panels or gallery-wrapped canvas in the size suited to your workshop. Pre-sealed and ready to pour — no prep needed from you.

Gold Leaf & Inclusions

24-carat gold and silver leaf flakes, crushed quartz crystals, dried botanical petals, and mica inclusions available at every workstation to elevate your piece.

Expert Artist Guide

Each session is led by one of our experienced resin artists with at least 3 years of teaching experience. Your guide roams the studio, offering personal coaching at every stage.

Refreshments & Atmosphere

Complimentary teas, coffees, soft drinks, and light snacks. Curated studio playlists, ambient lighting, and a relaxed creative vibe that makes the whole experience feel like a treat.
