Founded with Purpose

Our Story

From a small kitchen workshop to an award-winning resin art studio — here's how it all began.

It started in 2018 with a dining table, a bag of resin, and an unshakeable belief that beauty could be poured from a bottle. Our founder, Aria Mehta, began experimenting with pigments and resins after years in interior design — searching for something more tactile, more personal.

What began as weekend experiments quickly became a full studio, as word spread through the city about pieces that seemed to trap sunlight and colour in amber glass. By 2020, ResinLux had its first dedicated workshop space and a growing community of artists and collectors.

Today, our studio spans 3,000 sq ft of creative space, housing six artisans, three dedicated workshops, and a gallery that has featured in Architectural Digest, Elle Décor, and local art exhibitions across the country.

We exist to make meaningful art accessible, personal, and lasting — for every home and every story.

Fine art should not live only in galleries. We create bespoke pieces for everyday living spaces, making luxury tangible.

Looking Forward

Our Vision

To become the world's most beloved resin art studio — where every creation tells a story worth living with.

We envision a future where handcrafted resin art is as commonplace as fine photography — displayed in homes, offices, and public spaces around the globe. A future where our workshops have empowered over 10,000 aspiring artists to find their creative voice.

By 2030, we aim to have studios in five cities, a global online learning platform, and a certified sustainable supply chain that other studios model their practices after.
