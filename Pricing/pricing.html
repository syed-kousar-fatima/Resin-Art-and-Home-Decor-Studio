What You Get

Everything Included in Every Plan

A closer look at the features, resources, and studio access that make each ResinLux membership uniquely valuable.

Project Guide Library

Access a growing library of 80+ step-by-step resin project guides, from beginner coasters to advanced geode wall panels. New guides added every fortnight.

  • Starter: 2 guides/month
  • Studio: Unlimited access
  • Professional: Unlimited + early access

Live Q&A Sessions

Monthly live video sessions with ResinLux lead artists. Ask real questions in real time, watch live pours, and get personalised technique feedback directly from professionals.

  • Starter: 1 session/month
  • Studio: 4 sessions/month
  • Professional: Unlimited sessions

Studio Access Sessions

Book time in our 2,400 sq ft professional studio with full equipment access — pressure pots, orbital sanders, climate-controlled curing rooms, and 400+ pigments. The complete professional workspace, yours by appointment.

  • Starter: Not included
  • Studio: 2 sessions/month
  • Professional: Unlimited sessions

1-to-1 Artist Mentorship

Exclusive to Professional members: monthly one-on-one mentorship sessions with a dedicated ResinLux lead artist. Work on your portfolio, develop your signature style, and get honest critique that accelerates real growth.

  • Starter: Not included
  • Studio: Not included
  • Professional: 2 hours/month

Commission Discounts

Members save on all commissioned pieces ordered through the studio. Discounts scale with plan tier, applied automatically at checkout. Combined with your growing skill, the plan pays for itself fast.

  • Starter: No discount
  • Studio: 10% on all commissions
  • Professional: 25% on all commissions

Portfolio & Gallery Listing

Professional members receive a dedicated portfolio page on the ResinLux website, plus inclusion in our rotating gallery display at the studio — seen by hundreds of clients, press, and collaborators every month.

  • Starter: Not included
  • Studio: Not included
  • Professional: Full portfolio page
