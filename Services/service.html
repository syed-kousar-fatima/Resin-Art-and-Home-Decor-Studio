Our Process

How Every Creation Comes to Life

A seamless journey from first conversation to finished masterpiece delivered to your door.

01

Consultation

We discuss your vision, space, occasion, and budget to create a clear creative brief.

02

Design & Preview

Receive a digital colour mockup and approve the design before any resin is poured.

03

Artisan Crafting

Our artisans hand-pour, layer, and cure each piece over 48–72 hours for perfection.

04

Delivery & Install

Carefully packaged and delivered — with professional installation available for large pieces.

05

Aftercare

Dedicated aftercare support, cleaning guides, and a 12-month craftsmanship guarantee.
