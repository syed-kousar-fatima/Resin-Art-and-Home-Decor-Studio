Our Story

Crafting Beauty from
Liquid Resin

Founded in 2018, ResinCraft Studio began as a small passion project in a home garage and has grown into a thriving art studio creating bespoke resin pieces for homes and businesses worldwide.

Our team of dedicated artists creates each piece by hand, ensuring that every work carries our signature touch of quality and creativity. We believe every space deserves a unique piece of art.

How It Works

Our Creative Process

From concept to creation, every piece follows our meticulous 4-step artisan process.

01

Consultation

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your vision, space requirements, and style preferences.

02

Design & Planning

Our artists craft a detailed design concept, selecting colors, textures, and techniques that will bring your vision to life.

03

Creation

Using premium resins and hand-selected pigments, our artisans pour, swirl, and shape each piece with precision over 24–48 hours.

04

Delivery & Install

Your finished masterpiece is carefully packaged and delivered to your door. We also offer installation for large pieces.
